Who We Are

Building Stronger
Communities Since 2022

Shree Mithila Community Organization (SMCO) is a grassroots non-profit based in Chakraghatta Gaupalika, Sarlahi, Nepal. We work at the intersection of healthcare, education, and empowerment — bringing services directly to those who need them most.

Founded 2022, Chakraghatta Gaupalika, Sarlahi District, Nepal
Focus Education, Healthcare, Nutrition, Sanitation & Empowerment of marginalized populations
Vision Improve quality of life for underserved communities through sustainable interventions and partnerships
Location Chakraghatta-2, Sarlahi, Province No. 2, Nepal
Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A future where every individual in Sarlahi's underserved communities thrives — regardless of socioeconomic background — through sustainable, evidence-based interventions and inclusive partnerships that leave no one behind.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible healthcare, quality education, digital literacy, proper nutrition, and skill empowerment to marginalized groups in Chakraghatta Gaupalika — fostering dignity, resilience, and long-term self-sufficiency.

What We Do

Our SiX Program Areas

Six focused pillars that directly impact thousands of lives across Sarlahi every year.

Education

Increasing access for underprivileged children through scholarships, tutoring support, and enrollment drives that improve school retention in rural communities.

Digitalization

Empowering rural schools and youth with computer literacy programs — bridging the digital divide and unlocking opportunities for the next generation.

Healthcare & Nutrition

Expanding access through free health camps, awareness drives, maternal care, and targeted programs to combat child malnutrition across the district.

Empowerment

Fostering skill development and economic independence for women and marginalized groups through vocational training and entrepreneurship mentorship.

Agricultural Modernization

Supporting sustainable farming techniques and modern agricultural practices to improve yields, food security, and livelihoods for rural families.

Sanitation & Hygiene

Promoting clean water access, sanitation infrastructure, and hygiene education to reduce preventable disease and improve overall community health.
